About Us

For over a quarter of a century, NMS has been a trusted name in health care, long term care, educational and industrial foodservice management throughout Southwestern Ontario. In our years of providing service to our varied clients, we have enjoyed enviable client satisfaction.

NMS offers various levels of support range from consulting to total management. Our programs take an up-to-date approach with a full spectrum of service. NMS designs “tailor made” programs to meet our client’s needs allowing them to operate their foodservices effectively and efficiently. Visit the Services page for more details.

Your Foodservice Department will enjoy the advantage of having the top executives of Nutritional Management Services available directly to work on their projects. Our team of well trained, highly motivated professionals are ready to guide you through every aspect of Food Service.
