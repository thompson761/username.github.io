Career Opportunities

Current Openings

Operations/Sales Assistant

Location: Head Office

Description

We are currently seeking a dynamic individual who shares our philosophy of providing excellent food service management and value to our many clients. This exciting, full time opportunity offers the challenges and rewards second to none in this industry. Some travel within southern Ontario is required.

Requirements

  • Post secondary education in Food and Nutrition from a recognized university or college
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Member in good standing with the CSNM or eligibility
  • Strong food production skills and experience

How to Apply

Interested parties may submit a cover letter and resume to careers@nms.on.ca. Please indicate “NMS Head Office Opportunity 2014” in the subject line of your email.

Training

In partnership with Loyalist College, NMS can provide qualified instructors to coach your dietary staff through the MOHLTC mandate course to earn their Food Service Workers Certificate. The modules are taught on site allowing the staff to not only learn the curriculum but also learn about issues that are specific to your home.

Modules

  • Quantity Food Production
  • Sanitation & Safety
  • Institutional Food Service
  • Communication and the Food Service Worker
  • Introduction to Nutrition
  • Nutrition in Health Care
